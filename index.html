Welcome to Struvida Private Limited

Struvida is a specialist structural engineering consultancy focused exclusively on high-quality residential projects across England and India. Our practice is led by engineers holding an MSc in Structural Engineering accredited by the Institution of Structural Engineers (IStructE) and GIStructE designation.

We provide refined, regulation-compliant structural solutions that balance technical excellence with commercial efficiency. Our services include detailed 3D Revit structural models, delivered at competitive rates, along with clear, structured design data that enables builders and contractors to prepare accurate and efficient project quotations
